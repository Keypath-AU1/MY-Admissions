Student is on their final unit(s) or has just finished. Goal: convert confirmed intent into an actual submitted UNSW application before the window closes.

Opening & Purpose

2–3 minutes

Warm reconnect & compliance line

▾

Hi [Name], it's [Your Name] calling from SIM Online — great to catch up with you again! How has the been treating you so far?

[Tip: this is a warm call to an existing, paying student — lead with genuine rapport, not a sales tone. They already trust SIM; your job is to deepen that, not "pitch" from scratch.]
COMPLIANCE — REQUIRED BEFORE PROCEEDING

"Just a quick note that this call may be recorded for training and quality assurance purposes."

Purpose — early/mid-programme framing

▾

"The reason I'm calling today is I wanted to check in on how things are going, and also make sure you're aware of the pathway you're already on — every unit you're completing right now is actually building toward a UNSW Master's degree, at a 20% saving, if that's something you'd want to pursue. I'd love to walk you through what that could look like."

Purpose — late-stage framing

▾

"The reason I'm calling is that you're right at the point where the UNSW Master's articulation becomes real — you're eligible to move onto that pathway now, and I wanted to make sure we get your application in while everything's fresh and you don't lose momentum."

[Tip: create gentle urgency here — this is the highest-leverage moment in the entire student journey for this business outcome. Don't let the call end without a concrete next step.]

Objection Handling

as needed
⚠ "Even with 20% off, it's still expensive"
EMPATHISE
"That's fair — a Master's is a real investment, and it's right to think it through properly."
REFRAME
Reframe from cost to return: they've already absorbed the "startup cost" of postgraduate study with SIM — the Master's is the highest-leverage next step because it converts a specialist qualification into a globally recognised degree, at a lower combined cost than applying to UNSW without the pathway.
CLOSE
"I hear you. Compare it this way — you've already invested the time to get this far, and this discount only exists because of that. Walking away now means the specialist qualification stands alone; continuing gets you a full Master's for meaningfully less than starting from scratch. Does your employer have a training budget that could help offset the rest?"
⚠ "I'm tired, I need a break before starting something else"
EMPATHISE
"That's completely valid — you've been working hard, and that fatigue is real."
REFRAME
Don't pressure an immediate start — offer to lock in eligibility/pathway status now while deferring the actual UNSW start date, if that flexibility exists. CONFIRM whether deferred/later intake enrolment is possible under the articulation agreement before offering this.
CLOSE
"That makes complete sense — you don't have to decide on a start date today. What I'd suggest is we lock in your eligibility now, while everything's fresh, and you can choose when to actually begin. Would that take the pressure off?"
⚠ "I'm not sure I actually need a full Masters"
EMPATHISE
"That's a fair question to ask honestly — not everyone needs it, and I'd rather you go in for the right reason."
REFRAME
Connect back to the career goal they may have shared in earlier conversations — if they're targeting a role that expects a Master's-level qualification, or want to future-proof against more competitive candidates, this is the lowest-friction way to get there.
CLOSE
"Where do you see yourself heading in the next few years? [Listen] — if that path tends to reward a full Master's, this is genuinely the most cost- and time-efficient way to get one, since you're already most of the way there."
⚠ "I'm considering a different university for my Masters"
EMPATHISE
"That's a smart thing to weigh up — it's worth comparing your options."
REFRAME
Emphasise the unique advantage they'd be giving up: the 20% discount and direct unit credit only apply via this articulation pathway — enrolling elsewhere means starting a Master's from zero, at full cost and full duration.
CLOSE
"That's worth comparing, absolutely. Just so it's factored in — this pathway is the only option where the work you've already done actually counts toward the Master's, plus the 20% saving. Anywhere else, you'd likely be starting completely fresh. What's drawing you to the other option?"
⚠ "I'm worried my GPA or units won't qualify me"
EMPATHISE
"That's a really sensible thing to check before getting your hopes up."
REFRAME
Don't guess — pull their actual transcript/GPA from the CRM before or during the call. If they're short, identify exactly what's needed (a specific unit resit, grade improvement) rather than leaving it vague.
CLOSE
"Let's check that properly rather than guess — [review CRM/transcript]. Based on what I can see, [confirm eligibility or identify the specific gap and a plan to close it]." CONFIRM exact GPA/unit thresholds internally before stating eligibility to the student.
⚠ "I need to check with my employer / family first"
EMPATHISE
"Of course — that's a good habit before committing to something like this."
REFRAME
Support them for that conversation rather than treating it as a stall — offer a one-pager or the value case in writing, and lock a specific follow-up date.
CLOSE
"Makes sense. I can send you a short summary of the pathway and the discount over WhatsApp so you've got everything in front of you for that conversation. Can we pencil in a follow-up call for [specific date]?"

Closing & Next Steps

2–3 minutes

Lock in the concrete next step

▾

"So based on everything we've talked through, what I'd suggest as the next step is [submitting the UNSW application this week / confirming your unit selection / booking a follow-up once you've spoken to your employer] — how does that sound?"

→ Mandatory before ending the call:
  • Capture articulation intent in CRM as High / Medium / Low with the specific reason
  • Agree and log a specific date for the next action (application submission, document collection, or follow-up call)
  • If intent is High: send the UNSW application link / next steps via WhatsApp during the call, not after
